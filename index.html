Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who We Are

Dominion AI provides an AI-powered voice receptionist and messaging automation platform built for HVAC contractors in the United States. Contact: louay@dominionai.site.

2. Information We Collect

  • From contractors: Business name, contact name, phone, email, service area, and Google Calendar access.
  • From end-users: Name, phone, address, nature of HVAC issue, and appointment details collected during AI-assisted calls.
  • Automatically: Call recordings, duration, logs, and appointment data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and deliver the LeadLock AI voice agent service
  • To book appointments and send confirmation messages
  • To send automated SMS follow-up and review request messages on behalf of contractors
  • To generate monthly analytics reports
  • To improve our AI models and service quality

4. SMS Messaging — Consent, Frequency & Opt-Out

How you consent: When a homeowner provides their phone number to an HVAC contractor using Dominion AI — verbally, on a service agreement, or through an online inquiry — they consent to receive service-related text messages.

Message frequency: (1) one appointment confirmation per booking, (2) one missed-call follow-up per unanswered call, (3) one review request per completed job.

Opt out: Reply STOP at any time. You'll receive one final confirmation. Reply HELP for assistance.

Rates: Message and data rates may apply.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Telnyx LLC. — US phone numbers and SMS/voice infrastructure.
  • Vapi AI — Powers the AI voice agent.
  • Google Calendar API — Appointment booking and management.
  • n8n / Make — Workflow automation.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
