3. Fees and Billing

  • There is no setup fee and no monthly subscription.
  • The Client is billed $50 per appointment booked by the agent (Founding Clients: $35, locked for the lifetime of the account).
  • A "booked appointment" means a scheduled service or maintenance visit confirmed with the caller and written to the Client's calendar. Emergency escalations, information-only calls, spam calls and cancellations made by the caller within 24 hours are not billable.
  • Included call time: each account includes 1,500 minutes of answered call time per month. This is far more than a typical contractor uses — most accounts stay well under half of it. Time beyond the included allowance is billed at $0.25 per minute, and we will notify the Client before any overage is charged. This clause exists only to cover unusual cases, such as an entire main line being forwarded rather than overflow calls alone.
  • Invoices are issued monthly and itemize every billed appointment with its call recording. The Client may dispute any line item within 14 days, and disputed items are removed unless the recording clearly shows a booking.

  • Honor appointments booked by the agent, or notify us promptly if one cannot be kept.

5. Cancellation

Either party may cancel at any time with 14 days' written notice. On cancellation, any dedicated number is released and all automations are deactivated. The Client's data is exported on request within 30 days. Fees already earned for booked appointments remain payable.
