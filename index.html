Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who We Are

Dominion AI ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is a software services company that provides LeadLock, an AI-powered voice receptionist and messaging automation platform built for roofing contractors in the United States. Our registered business contact is: hello@dominionai.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • From contractors (our clients): Business name, contact name, phone number, email address, service area, and Google Calendar access credentials required to operate the LeadLock agent.
  • From end-users (homeowners who call contractors): Name, phone number, property address, nature of roofing inquiry, and appointment details — collected during AI-assisted phone calls on behalf of our contractor clients.
  • Automatically: Call recordings, call duration, call logs, and appointment data processed through our systems.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and deliver the LeadLock AI voice agent service
  • To book appointments and send confirmation messages
  • To send automated SMS follow-up messages on behalf of contractor clients
  • To send review request messages after job completion
  • To generate monthly call and lead reports for contractor clients
  • To improve our AI models and service quality

4. SMS Messaging — Consent, Frequency & Opt-Out

How you consent to receive SMS: When a homeowner provides their phone number to a roofing contractor using LeadLock — whether verbally, on a service agreement, or through an online inquiry form — they consent to receive service-related text messages. These messages include appointment confirmations, missed-call follow-ups, and post-job review requests sent on behalf of that contractor.

Message types and frequency: Message frequency varies based on your interaction with the contractor. You may receive: (1) one appointment confirmation SMS per booked appointment, (2) one missed-call follow-up SMS per unanswered call, and (3) one review request SMS per completed job.

How to opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. Reply HELP for assistance.

Rates: Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers to operate our service:

  • Twilio Inc. — Provides US phone numbers and SMS/voice infrastructure. Twilio processes call and message data in accordance with their Privacy Policy at twilio.com/legal/privacy.
  • Vapi AI — Powers the AI voice agent that handles inbound calls. Vapi processes call audio and transcripts.
  • Google Calendar API — Used to book and manage appointments on behalf of contractor clients. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • n8n / Make — Workflow automation platforms used to route data between services.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

Call logs and lead data are retained for 12 months and then deleted. Contractor account data is retained for the duration of the service agreement and deleted within 30 days of account termination upon request.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), access controls, and secure credential storage. We do not store full payment card information — payments are processed by our payment providers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@dominionai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Contact Us
