📱 Step-by-Step Guide
Call Forwarding Setup

Once you receive your agent number by email, follow the instructions below for your carrier. Takes less than 2 minutes.

Select your mobile carrier above to see the exact steps for your phone.
Carrier
AT&T
1

Open your Phone app and go to the dial pad.

2

Dial *61*[AGENT NUMBER]# — replace [AGENT NUMBER] with the number I sent to your email — then press Call.

3

You'll hear a short confirmation tone. That means forwarding is now active. Hang up.

4

Test it: call your business number from another phone and don't pick up. LeadLock should answer within 3 rings.

5

To turn off anytime: dial #61# and press Call. You'll hear a confirmation and it's deactivated.

This only forwards unanswered calls — if you pick up, the call goes to you as normal. LeadLock only activates when you miss it.
Carrier
Verizon
1

Open your Phone app, then tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.

2

Go to Settings → Calls → Call Forwarding.

3

Tap "Forward when unanswered" and enter your agent number from the email I sent you.

4

Tap Enable or Save. You'll see a confirmation on screen.

5

Test it: call your business number from another phone and don't pick up. LeadLock should answer within 3 rings.

Verizon alternative: If you don't see the menu option above, try dialing *71[AGENT NUMBER] from the dial pad and pressing Call. Some Verizon plans use this code instead.
This only forwards unanswered calls. If you pick up, the call goes to you normally.
